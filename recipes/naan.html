Naan

Description

You will die before you taste anything better than naan. It is the tortilla's great-great-great-great-great-great grandparent but way better because it is thicker, savorier, and can be used as a pizza, flatbread, or even a bed.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 cup AP flour, plus more for rolling (see Notes)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp kalonji (optional)
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds (optional)
  • Melted butter to brush onto naan (to taste)
  • Coarse sea salt to sprinkle onto naan (to taste)
